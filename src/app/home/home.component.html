Cholesterol check

High cholesterol does not cause symptoms. You can only find out if you have it from a blood test. Your GP might suggest having a test if they think your cholesterol level could be high. This may because of your age, weight or another condition you have (like high blood pressure or diabetes). There are 2 ways of having a cholesterol test: Taking blood from your arm or Finger-prick test.
